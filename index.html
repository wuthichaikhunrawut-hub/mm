Heart Disease Risk Factors

Heart disease is significantly influenced by clinical variables such as high cholesterol, hypertension, abnormal chest pain patterns, and exercise-induced ischemia. The engine analyzes these metrics to provide a calculated probability of cardiac issues.

*This system is for educational purposes and should not be used as a primary diagnostic tool.
